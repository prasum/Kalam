Our Mission

"KALAM" has been founded to serve the most deprived children of the country , that is, those living in rural areas and urban slums. "Kalam" focus on sending these children to schools to have access of education , food, books, etc. It is managed by a devoted team of social workers in co-operation & collaboration with a wide range of individuals and NGO's.
